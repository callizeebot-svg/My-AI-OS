# Work: Liverpool Hospital — Mental Health

## Workplace

- **Hospital**: Liverpool Hospital
- **District**: South West Sydney Local Health District (SWSLHD)
- **Department**: Mental health (inpatient)
- **Role**: Social worker

## What My Work Involves

- Discharge planning for mental health inpatients
- Navigating complex social circumstances: housing, family support, community services
- Non-citizen/non-PR patients face additional barriers (limited Medicare, visa status, no safety net)
- Coordinating with external agencies: NDIA, housing, immigration, family, community mental health teams
- Risk assessments, care plan contributions, family meetings

## Key Challenges I Work With

- Patients who can't be discharged because they have no safe housing
- Patients without Medicare or income support — system doesn't know what to do with them
- Long stays due to social rather than clinical reasons
- Burnout risk for social workers in this setting

## How Betty Can Help My Work

- Draft complex letters and reports faster
- Track patient progress notes and key contacts (when appropriate)
- Help navigate policy questions (NDIS, Medicare, visa pathways)
- Manage my calendar and team communication
- Reduce admin burden so I have more energy for direct client work

## Important Note on Privacy

Never store identifiable patient information in Betty or My-AI-OS. Work context here is about my role, not about specific clients.
