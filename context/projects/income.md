# Project: Income Generation

- **Target**: $10k/month within 6 months (by ~Nov 2026)
- **Vision**: $1M/year from online business (multi-stream)
- **Status**: Early stage — building the tooling foundation (Betty, AI-OS)

## Strategy

Build first, monetize what works. Don't pick a revenue stream before having something people want.

## Income Stream Ideas (exploring)

1. **AI tools for social sector** — Betty-like systems for social workers, community health, mental health
2. **Automation consulting** — help small organisations implement AI workflows
3. **Productized Betty** — SaaS version of Betty OS for health/human services professionals
4. **Content/education** — teach others how to build with Claude and AI agents
5. **Freelance AI builds** — one-off agent-native apps for clients

## Current Blockers

- Betty OS not yet production-ready (close)
- No paying customers yet
- Need to validate which income stream has real demand
