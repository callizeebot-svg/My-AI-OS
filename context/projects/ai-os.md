## What It Is

A full copy of BuilderIO's agent-native framework. Templates for 19 apps where the UI and AI agent share the same database. Apps you own and can fork/customize.

## Templates Available

`dispatch` · `mail` · `calendar` · `content` · `voice` · `forms` · `analytics` · `slides` · `clips` · `design` · `videos` · `meeting-notes` · `images` · `calls` · `macros` · `issues` · `recruiting` · `scheduling` · `starter`

## Relationship to My-AI-OS

- `AI-OS` = the engine (don't touch)
- `My-AI-OS` = my personal workspace (where I customize and run apps)
- When agent-native releases updates, sync them: `scripts/sync-upstream.sh`
